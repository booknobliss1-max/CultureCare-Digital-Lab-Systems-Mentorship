The Digital Steward’s First Audit

Before we build, we must understand the ground we stand on.

01. Sovereignty

Question: Who owns the server where your data lives? If the company vanished tomorrow, would your work vanish with it?

02. Stewardship of Attention

Question: Does this digital tool respect your focus, or is it designed to harvest your "scroll"? A steward protects the mind as much as the data.

03. The Lineage (Provenance)

Question: Where did the code or the AI model come from? Was it built on exploited labor, or was it grown out of community care?

04. Accessibility (The Wide Door)

Question: Can a person with limited vision or restricted motor skills use this? A digital home isn't a home if the door is too narrow.

05. Ecological Footprint

Question: Is this system "heavy"? Does it require massive amounts of energy to run, or is it lightweight and sustainable?

Mentor’s Note: Stewardship is not about perfection; it is about intentionality. We audit so we can choose our compromises wisely.
